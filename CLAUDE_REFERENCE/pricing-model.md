# AUTONYX — Pricing Model

> Document version: April 2026 | AUTONYX / BORIS Killer project

AUTONYX is purpose-built for Australian fire protection and trades businesses. Unlike generic tools such as Tradify or Fergus, AUTONYX includes AS1851 compliance workflows, building/level/room job structure, Fair Work pay rules, Xero integration, and a customer portal — features that justify a premium price point while remaining 25–35% cheaper than the closest specialist competitor, AroFlo.

## Extra Seat Pricing Logic

Extra seat pricing decreases as customers move up tiers — rewarding growth and creating a natural incentive to upgrade. Customers on higher tiers get progressively cheaper per-seat costs, making AUTONYX more competitive the larger a business grows.

| Tier | Extra seat price | AroFlo equivalent | AUTONYX advantage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $60 / user / month | $67 / user | 10% cheaper |
| Pro | $50 / user / month | $67 / user | 25% cheaper |
| Business | $45 / user / month | $67 / user | 33% cheaper |
| Enterprise | $40 / user / month | $67 / user | 40% cheaper |

## Competitive Positioning

- **Primary benchmark: AroFlo.** Australian-founded, same target market. AUTONYX targets 25–35% cheaper at every team size.
- **Not compared to Tradify or Fergus.** Those are generic tradie tools. AUTONYX includes fire protection compliance, AS1851 workflows, and Xero payroll integration that those products do not offer.
- **Uptick context.** Uptick is the dominant fire protection specialist in Australia. Their pricing is custom/opaque but estimated at $90+/user. At 15 users, AUTONYX Business ($749) is approximately 44% cheaper than Uptick (~$1,350).
- **30-day trial advantage.** Most competitors (Tradify, Fergus, ServiceM8) offer 14-day trials only. Enterprise tools (AroFlo, Simpro, Uptick) offer no trial at all. 30 days is a meaningful conversion differentiator.
- **Annual billing.** 15% discount on Starter/Pro, 20% on Business. Enterprise pricing is negotiated per contract.
